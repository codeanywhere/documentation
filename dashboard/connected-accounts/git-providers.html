Support

Git Providers

If you want to work seamlessly on your Git repositories, you have to connect your Git provider account and your Codeanywhere account in the Connected accounts section of the Account page.

GitHub

You will be redirected to the Github authorization page to where you will have to confirm your identity and select the Authorize Codeanywhere option.

Bitbucket

You will be redirected to the Bitbucket authorization page to where you will have to confirm your identity and select the Authorize Codeanywhere option.

GitLab

If you wish to connect your Codeanywhere account to your GitLab account, follow GitLabs official guide found here.

Note: The public key that you have to add to the GitLab account can be found in the Accounts tab in your dashboard.
